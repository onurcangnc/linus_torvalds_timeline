Here is a time line of Linus Torvalds's life:

  • 1969 - Born in Helsinki

  • 1981 - Begins writing code

  • 1988 - Attends University of Helsinki

  • 1989 - Joins Finnish Army

  • 1991 - Receives his first copy of UNIX

  • 1991 - First Linux Prototypes publicly released

  • 1994 - Linux version 1.0 released

  • 1996 - Finishes his Master of Science thesis

  • 2000 - Worked at Transmeta Corporation

  • 2012 - Inducted into the Internet Hall of Fame
